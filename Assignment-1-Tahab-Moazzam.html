Tahab Moazzam

Tahab Moazzam is the passionate owner of "Crafting Cafe" located in University of Houston - Sugar Land.

With an extensive background in culinary arts and a love for crafting new and original dishes, Tahab brings a never before seen uniqueness to the restaurant's menu.

Before opening the UOH Sugarland Cafeteria, Tahab gained valuable experience working in renowned restaurants and hotels, honing his skills in both traditional and contemporary cooking techniques.

Driven by a desire to provide exceptional dining experiences to his customers, Tahab ensures that every dish served at the UOH Sugarland Cafeteria is made with the freshest ingredients and prepared with meticulous attention to detail.

Outside of the kitchen, Tahab enjoys spending time with his family, exploring new culinary trends, and engaging with the local community.
